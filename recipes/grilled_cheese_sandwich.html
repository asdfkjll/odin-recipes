Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

It's a grilled cheese sandwich

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side each bread slice
  3. Place 1 slice bread, butter-side down, in the hot skillet; top with 1 slice cheese
  4. Place 1 slice bread, butter-side up, on top cheese
  5. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip and continue cooking until cheese is melted
  6. Repeat with remaining bread and cheese slices. Serve and enjoy!
